Privacy Policy for Keto Fit

Last updated: May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Keto Fit collects, uses, and protects user information. By using Keto Fit, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Keto Fit may collect the following information:

  • Name — if provided by the user for account or profile functionality.
  • Email address — used for account login, authentication, and account-related functionality.
  • Purchase history — used to manage subscriptions, verify premium access, and restore purchases.
  • Crash data — used to identify, diagnose, and fix app stability issues.

How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To authenticate users and provide app functionality.
  • To verify subscription status and provide access to premium features.
  • To restore purchases.
  • To improve app stability and fix crashes or technical issues.

Subscriptions and Purchases

Keto Fit uses RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and purchase validation. RevenueCat may process purchase-related information, such as subscription status, purchase history, product identifiers, and app user identifiers, in order to provide subscription functionality.

Crash Reporting

Keto Fit uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic information. This information helps us understand and fix crashes, bugs, and performance-related issues.

Analytics and Tracking

Keto Fit does not use collected personal information for third-party advertising or tracking. We do not sell user personal information.

Data Linked to User Identity

Some information, such as name, email address, purchase history, and subscription status, may be linked to the user's account or app user identifier when required for app functionality.

Data Sharing

We may share limited information with trusted third-party service providers that help us operate the app, including RevenueCat for subscription management and Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting. These services process data only as needed to provide their functionality.

Data Retention

We retain user information only as long as necessary to provide the app's functionality, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain app security and stability.

User Rights

Users may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of their personal information, subject to applicable laws and technical limitations.

Children's Privacy

Keto Fit is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
